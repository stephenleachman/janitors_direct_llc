Maintenance Cleaning Recurring Cleaning

Our primary service, maintenance cleaning, is a scheduled, reoccurring cleaning service customized to each client's needs. We've designed this service to maintain and keep your facility clean and sanitized.

Maintenance cleaning focuses on critical areas that matter most to you, such as high-traffic areas, break rooms and restrooms. When we perform an initial walk-through of your facility, we help identify these areas. We will form a detailed scope of work, ensuring we can exceed the most demanding expectations.

Below is a list of general cleaning tasks performed within all facilities. Based on your company's industry and facilities needs, we will add specific cleaning tasks to the list below.

  • Trash bins emptied & liners replaced
  • Sweep, mop, vacuum
  • Light dusting
  • Corners of ceiling dusted/Remove cobwebs
  • Door handles sanitized
  • Entry door(s) glass cleaned
  • Janitor's closet cleaned/organized
  • Keep inventory of janitor's supplies

  • Clean microwave(s)
  • Disinfect all counters & tables
  • Clean & shine sink(s)
  • Clean the exterior of refrigerator(s)
  • Replenished hand towels
  • Replenished hand/dish soap

  • Clean and disinfect Toilets & Urinals
  • Clean and disinfect stall doors & Walls
  • Replace & keep toilet paper stocked
  • Sinks and counters cleaned and sanitized
  • Meers cleaned
  • Replenished hand towels
  • Replenished hand soap
